Support a ban on proctoring software!

MathSoc is advocating for a ban on proctoring software. Simply put: it does little to prevent cheating, and it is a massive invasion of privacy. We are collecting signatures to prove that this has support. We are already near 1000 signatures, but the more we can get the more force the petition will have. Anyone at Waterloo can sign, even if they are not in the Math Faculty.

Sign the petition!
